# **App Name**: AlquilaGestión Pro

## Core Features:

- Resumen Dashboard: Visualize key metrics like current financials, tenant status (overdue, current, legal), and maintenance progress at a glance.
- Gestión de Propiedades: Add, edit, and manage rental properties, including details like address, owner information, and unit specifics.
- Gestión de Inquilinos y Contratos: Maintain a comprehensive database of tenants, their contact information, assigned properties, contract expiration dates, and upload scanned contract documents.
- Gestión de Facturas y Servicios: Record and track utility bills, communal expenses, and other service-related invoices. Mark them as paid/pending and attach receipt files.
- Registro de Mantenimiento: Log and track maintenance tasks, expenses, assign statuses (pending, in progress, completed), and upload relevant documentation (e.g., invoices, photos).
- Casos Legales y Mediaciones: Manage records of legal cases or mediation processes related to properties or tenants, including status, associated attorney, and important dates.
- Asistente de Comunicación IA: Leverage a generative AI tool to draft customized messages or emails for common landlord-tenant communications, such as rent reminders, lease renewal notices, or owner liquidation reports.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A warm, sophisticated burnt orange (#D8822B) to convey efficiency and trustworthiness.
- Background color: A very subtle pale cream (#FCF5F0), almost off-white, providing a clean and light backdrop.
- Accent color: A rich terracotta red (#BE3610) to highlight important actions or draw attention to critical information.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for its modern, clear, and highly legible characteristics, suitable for data-rich dashboards.
- Utilize a consistent set of clean, line-style icons (like those from Lucide) to enhance visual clarity and user comprehension across the interface.
- Employ a responsive dashboard layout featuring a collapsible sidebar for navigation, organizing content into clearly defined cards and tables for structured data presentation.
- Incorporate subtle, tasteful animations for transitions between sections, modal interactions, and feedback on interactive elements like buttons and forms, ensuring a fluid user experience.
